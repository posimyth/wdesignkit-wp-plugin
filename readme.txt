== Description ==

WDdesignKit is a powerful WordPress plugin that offers a wide range of website templates and custom widgets/blocks for both Elementor and Gutenberg. With a collection of over 100 templates and more than 20 custom widgets/blocks for each page builder, WDdesignKit is designed to enhance your website building experience and provide you with the tools you need to create stunning websites with ease.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Will This Work With All Themes ? =
Yes this works with all the Theme, but it works best in sync with our Nexter WordPress Theme.

= Is Widget Builder free to use? =
Yes. Our Elementor Widget Builder, Upcoming Gutenberg Blocks builder and future Builders will be 100% free to use. There are some limitations on the number of widgets you can create in the free plan.

= What will existing POSIMYTH Product users receive? =
If you have The Plus Addons for Elementor, The Plus Blocks for Gutenberg or Nexter WP Theme, You need to use that key in the activation panel of WDesignKit. That will give you instant access of all Pro Templates and Pro Widgets, (Respective to Plugin you have, If you have The Plus Addons for Elementor, You will get access of all Elementor Related Pro Templates & Widgets). In addition, you will receive additional credits, which you can utilise to store or manage your Templates, Widgets and/or Workspaces on cloud.

= When is the exit from the beta phase planned? =
We are working on several improvements on background and seeking feedback from public beta users to make it even more feature rich and stable before going live.

= Can I save templates from both Elementor Free and Pro using WDesignKit? =
Yes, You can save and use Elementor Free and Pro both templates.
